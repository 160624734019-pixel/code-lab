Why early prediction matters

Hypertension (high blood pressure) is a silent killer affecting 1.28 billion adults. Traditional monitors only show current reading — they cannot predict future risk. PulseGuard AI fills that gap.

  • 43% of hypertensive adults are unaware of their condition.
  • Early intervention can reduce cardiovascular events by up to 30%.
  • Most devices don't give personalized trend forecast.

Reactive vs. Predictive

PulseGuard AI uses ML to spot risk before BP spikes.
